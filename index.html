Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

The legendary Maratha warrior and founder of the Maratha Empire, known for his bravery, leadership, and vision.

Here's a time line of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's life:

  • 1630 - Born in the hill-port of Shivneri, Maharastra, to Shahaji Bhosale and Jijabai. His mother instills strong values of justice, ledership, and devotion to Hindu culture.
  • 1645 - At the age of 15, he starts taking control of forts in the Pune region, setting the foundation for the Maratha Empire.
  • 1647 - Takes control of Torna Fort, his first major conquest, followed by Rajgad Fort, which becomes the capital.
  • 1659 - Dfeats Afzal Khan, a powerful general of the Adilshahi Sultanate, usig brilliant military strategy. Expands Maratha influence significantlly.
  • 1664 - Raids Surat, a wealthy mughal trading city, to weaken their economy and fund his growing army.
  • 1665 - Signs the Treaty of Purandar with the Mughals, surrendering some forts but continuing guerrilla warfare.
  • 1666 - Cleverly escapes from Agra, where he was held captive by Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb. This becomes one of the most daring escapes in Indian history.
  • 1670 - Recaptures forts lost to the Mughals, marking the beginning of a full-fledged war against them.
  • 1674 - Coronated as Chhatrapati(Emperor) of the Maratha Empire at Raigad Fort.
  • 1680 - Passes away, leaving behind a strong Maratha Empire that would challenge the Mughals for decades.
